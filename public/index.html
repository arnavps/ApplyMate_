      How to Use ApplyMate

      1. Upload your resume in PDF format

      4. Review your match score, cover letter, and interview questions
      5. Use the suggestions to improve your resume

      Frequently Asked Questions

      What file formats are supported?

      Currently, only PDF files are supported for resume uploads.

      How accurate is the match score?

      The match score is calculated using AI analysis of skills, experience, and qualifications. It provides a good estimate but should be used as a guide.

      Are my analyses saved?

      Yes, if you're signed in, all your analyses are saved and can be accessed from the "My Applications" page.
